What is this?

This is an implementation of the URL shortening project, shortener_project.

What are it's current features?

  • base64 conversion of link primary keys.
  • Custom shortened link names like '/django-project/'.
  • Gathers link metrics but preserves the anonymity of the user.

What are it's planned features?

  • geoplacement of users.
  • Fancy dashboard charts and graphs.
  • Incorporation into a forthcoming Django tutorial.
